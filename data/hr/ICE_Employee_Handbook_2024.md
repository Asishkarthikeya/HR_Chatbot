# ICE Employee Handbook 2024

## Core Working Hours

All employees at the Atlanta headquarters are expected to be available during core working hours: **8:00 AM – 5:00 PM Eastern Standard Time (EST)**. Flexible scheduling is permitted outside of core hours, provided your total weekly hours meet the 40-hour requirement and your manager approves your schedule.

## Work Location Policy

ICE requires **full-time on-site presence** for this role:

- **5 days in-office** (Monday through Friday) at the Atlanta headquarters
- Remote work is **not available** for this position
- All employees must be physically present at the office during core working hours
- Employees must badge in at the building entrance each day
- Any exceptions (e.g., medical accommodation) require written approval from your VP and HR Business Partner

## Dress Code

- **Business casual** is the standard dress code for all office days
- No jeans, sneakers, or athletic wear on **client-facing days** (check your team calendar)
- On non-client days, dark jeans with a collared shirt are acceptable
- All employees must wear their ICE badge visibly while on-premises

## Paid Time Off (PTO)

| Tenure | Annual PTO Days |
|--------|----------------|
| Year 1–2 | 15 days |
| Year 3–5 | 20 days |
| Year 6+ | 25 days |

- PTO requests must be submitted in Workday at least **2 weeks in advance** for planned absences
- PTO does not roll over beyond 5 unused days per year
- Managers may deny PTO during critical project phases or release windows

## Sick Leave

- **10 sick days per year**, available from your start date
- Sick leave does **not roll over** to the next calendar year
- A doctor's note is required for absences exceeding **3 consecutive days**
- Sick leave cannot be used as supplemental PTO

## Health Insurance

- Enrollment must be completed within **30 days of your start date** via Workday
- **Plan options:**
  - Aetna PPO (individual or family coverage)
  - Aetna High-Deductible Health Plan (HDHP) with HSA option
- ICE contributes up to **$1,500/year** to employee HSA accounts on the HDHP plan
- Dental and vision coverage are included in all plans
- Coverage is effective on the **first day of the month following your start date**

## 401(k) Retirement Plan

- ICE offers a **401(k) plan through Fidelity**
- Company match: **6% of salary**, dollar-for-dollar
- Matching begins after **90 days of employment**
- Employees are immediately vested in their own contributions; company match vests over **3 years**
- Enrollment and fund selection are managed through Fidelity NetBenefits

## Employee Assistance Program (EAP)

- Free, confidential counseling services available 24/7
- Covers mental health, financial planning, legal consultations, and work-life balance support
- Contact: 1-800-555-0199 or via the Workday benefits portal
