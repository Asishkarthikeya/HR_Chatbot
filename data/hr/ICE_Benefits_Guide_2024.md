# ICE Benefits Guide 2024

## Benefits Eligibility

- All full-time employees (30+ hours/week) are eligible for the full benefits package
- Benefits enrollment must be completed within **30 days of your start date** via Workday
- If you miss the enrollment window, you must wait until **Open Enrollment** (November) for the following year
- Qualifying life events (marriage, birth, divorce, loss of other coverage) allow mid-year changes — notify HR within 30 days

### Which Plan Should I Choose?
- **PPO** is better if: You have frequent doctor visits, take brand-name medications, prefer predictable copays, or have a family with ongoing medical needs
- **HDHP** is better if: You're generally healthy, want lower premiums, want to take advantage of the HSA tax benefits, or you want ICE's $1,500 HSA contribution

### Coverage Start Date
- Coverage begins on the **first day of the month following your start date**
- Example: If you start on March 15, your coverage begins April 1

## Health Savings Account (HSA)

- Available **only** with the HDHP plan
- ICE contributes **$1,500/year** to your HSA (deposited quarterly: $375/quarter)
- 2024 IRS contribution limits: **$4,150** (individual) / **$8,300** (family) — includes ICE's contribution
- Contributions are **pre-tax**, growth is **tax-free**, and withdrawals for qualified medical expenses are **tax-free** (triple tax advantage)
- HSA funds roll over year to year — they never expire
- HSA is administered through **Fidelity** (same platform as 401k)
- You own your HSA — it stays with you even if you leave ICE

## Flexible Spending Account (FSA)

- Available with the **PPO plan only** (IRS rules prohibit having both FSA and HSA)
- **Healthcare FSA:** Contribute up to **$3,200/year** pre-tax for medical, dental, and vision expenses
- **Dependent Care FSA:** Contribute up to **$5,000/year** pre-tax for childcare or elder care expenses
- **Use-it-or-lose-it:** FSA funds expire on December 31 each year (ICE allows a $640 rollover for Healthcare FSA)
- FSA is administered through **WageWorks**

## Life Insurance & Disability

### Life Insurance
- **Basic life insurance:** ICE provides **2x your annual salary** at no cost to you (up to $500,000)
- **Supplemental life insurance:** You may purchase additional coverage up to 5x salary through payroll deduction
- **Spouse/dependent life insurance:** Available for purchase at group rates

### Disability Coverage
- **Short-term disability (STD):** 60% of salary for up to 26 weeks — provided by ICE at no cost
- **Long-term disability (LTD):** 60% of salary after 26 weeks — provided by ICE at no cost
- Disability benefits coordinate with any state disability benefits and Social Security Disability Insurance
- Elimination period: 7 days for illness, 0 days for injury

## 401(k) Retirement Plan

- Provider: **Fidelity**
- Company match: **6% of salary**, dollar-for-dollar
- Match eligibility begins after **90 days of employment**
- Vesting: Your contributions are always 100% vested. Company match vests over **3 years** (33% per year):
  - Year 1: 33% vested
  - Year 2: 66% vested
  - Year 3: 100% vested
- 2024 IRS contribution limits: **$23,000** (under 50) / **$30,500** (50 and older)
- **Roth 401(k)** option is available (post-tax contributions, tax-free withdrawals in retirement)
- Enrollment and fund selection: **Fidelity NetBenefits** (fidelity.com/netbenefits)
- You can change contribution percentages at any time
- Default investment if you don't choose: Target Date Fund based on your estimated retirement year

## Commuter Benefits

- **MARTA subsidy:** $50/month for public transit passes (enroll through Workday)
- **Parking:** Free employee parking at the Atlanta office garage
- **Pre-tax transit benefits:** You may set aside up to $315/month pre-tax for transit expenses beyond the MARTA subsidy
- **Bicycle commuters:** $20/month reimbursement for bike commuting expenses (maintenance, storage)

## Additional Benefits

### Pet Insurance
- Nationwide pet insurance available at group rates through payroll deduction
- Covers dogs and cats — illness, injury, and wellness plans available
- Enroll any time through Workday Benefits

### Legal Insurance
- MetLife Legal Plans available through payroll deduction (~$20/month)
- Covers: Will preparation, real estate closings, estate planning, traffic violations, family law consultations
- Enroll during Open Enrollment or within 30 days of hire

### Identity Theft Protection
- Norton LifeLock available at group rates (~$10/month)
- Covers: Credit monitoring, identity restoration, up to $1M insurance
- Enroll through Workday Benefits

### Employee Stock Purchase Plan (ESPP)
- Purchase ICE stock (NYSE: ICE) at a **10% discount** through payroll deductions
- Contribution limit: Up to **10% of base salary** per pay period
- Purchase windows: Every 6 months (June 30 and December 31)
- Enrollment opens 2 weeks before each purchase window
- Shares are deposited into your Fidelity brokerage account
