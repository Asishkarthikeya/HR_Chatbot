# ICE Atlanta Office Information

## Office Address

**Intercontinental Exchange — Atlanta Headquarters**
5660 New Northside Drive NW, 3rd Floor
Atlanta, GA 30328

## Getting to the Office

### Parking
- Free employee parking is available in the attached garage (Levels P2 and P3)
- Use your employee badge to access the parking gate
- Visitor parking is on Level P1 — notify Security in advance if expecting guests

### Public Transit
- The office is accessible via MARTA (Sandy Springs station, ~10 min shuttle)
- ICE provides a monthly MARTA subsidy of **$50** through the commuter benefits program in Workday

## Badge Access

- Your employee badge grants access to the building, parking garage, and your assigned floor
- Badge must be worn visibly at all times while on-premises
- Lost badges: Report immediately to Security (ext. 2-1000) and submit a replacement request via ServiceNow
- After-hours access (before 7 AM or after 7 PM) requires manager approval in the access control system

## Cafeteria & Dining

- **ICE Café** (Ground Floor): Open 7:30 AM – 2:00 PM, Monday through Friday
- Coffee bar with complimentary coffee, tea, and water available all day
- Subsidized lunch options ranging from $5–$10
- Microwaves and refrigerators available in break rooms on each floor

### Nearby Lunch Options
- Chick-fil-A — 0.3 miles
- Chipotle — 0.5 miles
- The Battery Atlanta (dining & entertainment district) — 1.2 miles
- Various options in the Powers Ferry shopping area — 0.4 miles

## Conference Room Booking

- All conference rooms are booked through **Outlook Calendar**
- Naming convention: Rooms are named after rivers (Hudson, Thames, Ganges, Rhine, etc.)
- Each room has a display panel outside showing current availability
- Large meetings (10+ people) should use the "Exchange Hall" on the 1st floor — book at least 48 hours in advance
- Video conferencing equipment (Zoom Rooms) is available in all medium and large rooms

## Equipment Requests

- Standard setup: **MacBook Pro 16"** (or Dell XPS 15 if preferred), two monitors, keyboard, mouse, headset
- Equipment is provided on Day 1 by IT Support (Room 2-104)
- Additional hardware (extra monitor, standing desk, ergonomic chair): Submit a **ServiceNow** request under "Facilities → Equipment Request"
- Ergonomic assessments are available through HR — email jessica.martinez@ice-internal.com to schedule
- Software licenses (JetBrains, Postman Pro, etc.): Request through your manager → ServiceNow approval workflow
- All equipment must be returned upon offboarding

## Expense Reimbursement

- All expense reports are submitted through **Concur**
- Receipts are required for any expense over **$25**
- Manager approval is required for all submissions
- Reimbursement processing time: **5–7 business days** after manager approval
- Corporate credit card: Apply through the Finance team after 90 days of employment
- Travel policy: Book through Concur Travel; flights over $500 require VP approval
