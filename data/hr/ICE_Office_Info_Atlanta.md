## Getting to the Office

### Driving Directions
- From I-285: Take exit 22 (New Northside Drive). The office is 0.5 miles south on the left.
- From I-75: Take exit 256 (Mt. Paran Road/Northside Drive). Head east on Northside Drive for 1.2 miles.
- GPS address: 5660 New Northside Drive NW, Atlanta, GA 30328

### Parking
- Free employee parking is available in the attached garage (Levels P2 and P3)
- Use your employee badge to access the parking gate
- Visitor parking is on Level P1 — notify Security (ext. 2-1000) in advance if expecting guests
- Motorcycle and bicycle parking is available on Level P1 near the elevator
- Electric vehicle charging stations: 4 spots on Level P2 (first come, first served, free)

### Public Transit
- The office is accessible via MARTA (Sandy Springs station, ~10 min shuttle)
- ICE provides a monthly **MARTA subsidy of $50** through the commuter benefits program in Workday
- The ICE shuttle runs between Sandy Springs station and the office every 15 minutes during peak hours (7:30–9:30 AM, 4:30–6:30 PM)
- Uber/Lyft drop-off: Use the main entrance on New Northside Drive

## Badge Access

- Your employee badge grants access to the building, parking garage, and your assigned floor
- Badge must be worn **visibly at all times** while on-premises
- Lost badges: Report immediately to Security (ext. 2-1000) and submit a replacement request via ServiceNow — a temporary badge will be issued same day
- After-hours access (before 7:00 AM or after 7:00 PM): Requires manager approval in the access control system
- Weekend access: Available with manager approval — contact Security 24 hours in advance
- Tailgating (letting someone follow you through a badge door) is a security violation — always ensure the door closes behind you

## Emergency Procedures

- **Fire alarm:** Evacuate immediately via the nearest stairwell (do not use elevators). Assembly point is the parking garage Level P1 entrance.
- **Medical emergency:** Call ext. 2-9111 (Security) and 911. First aid kits are located in every break room. AED defibrillators are on each floor near the elevators.
- **Severe weather (tornado warning):** Move to interior rooms on the lowest floor. Designated shelter areas are marked with signs.
- **Active threat:** Follow "Run, Hide, Fight" protocol. Alert Security at ext. 2-1000 immediately.
- **Building evacuation routes:** Posted near all exits and elevators. Review these during your first-week facilities tour.

## Visitor Policy

- All visitors must be pre-registered with Security (ext. 2-1000) at least 24 hours in advance
- Visitors receive a temporary badge at Reception and must be escorted at all times
- No visitor access to server rooms, restricted floors (4th floor executive area), or secure areas
- Visitor meeting rooms on the Ground Floor do not require escort
- Visitor Wi-Fi: Available (no VPN access) — credentials provided at Reception
